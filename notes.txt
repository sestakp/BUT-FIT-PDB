# READ API OPERATIONS AND THEIR PROJECTION IN READ SERVICE:

POST	/api/customers - CreateCustomerMessage ✅️
	- Customers

PUT	/api/customers/{customerId} - UpdateCustomerMessage ✅️
	- Customers

DELETE	/api/customers/{customerId} ✅️
	- Customers
	- Orders - zmazanie všetkých objednávok daného customera

POST	/api/customers/{customerId}/addresses - AddCustomerAddressMessage ✅️
	- Customers

PUT	/api/customers/{customerId}/addresses/{addressId} - UpdateCustomerAddressMessage ✅️
	- Customers

DELETE	/api/customers/{customerId}/addresses/{addressId} - DeleteCustomerAddressMessage ✅️
	- Customers

POST	/api/orders ❓️

POST	/api/orders/{orderId}/add-to-cart/{productId} ❓️

PUT	/api/orders/{orderId}/complete - OrderCompletedMessage ❓️
	- Orders

POST	/api/products - CreateProductMessage ✅️
	- Products
	- Products of category
	- Products of subcategory
	- Vendors - lebo pri vytvarani productu sa špecifikuju kategories a vendor ma na sebe všetky catgories kt. ponúka

POST	/api/products/{productId}/reviews - CreateReviewMessage ✅️ - TESTING REQUIRED

DELETE	/api/products/{productId} - DeleteProductMessage ✅️ - TESTING REQUIRED

POST	/api/vendors - CreateVendorMessage ✅️
	- Vendors

PUT	/api/vendors/{vendorId} ✅️

	- Products - lebo tam je embedded vendor v ramci kt. je meno
	- Products of category - lebo tam je embedded vendor v ramci kt. je meno

DELETE	/api/vendors/{vendorId} ✅️
